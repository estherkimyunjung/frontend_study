Web Design Trends for 2019

Speed - You have less than three seconds to make a good impression with a potential customer
Flat Design - The quick-loading websites, minimalism and usability
Mobile First - With mobile sites taking priority away from desktop sites
Broken Grid/Asymmetrical Layouts - The designs that keep the user from focusing on the most important parts of the page.
Shapes - Shapes are like colors in that there are thoughts and emotions that people naturally associate with each one.
Single Page Design - The websites that have all content in only one page.
Video Backgrounds - Videos are simply more compelling than text or images ever will be.
Micro-Animations - Help the user know that they’re doing the right actions as they progress.
Chatbots/Machine Learning - Provide excellent customer service upfront
